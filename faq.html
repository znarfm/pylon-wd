Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pylon Esports?

Pylon Esports is the official esports organization of PUP, dedicated to fostering competitive gaming and building a community of student gamers.

How can I join Pylon Esports?

Any PUP student can join! Simply:

  • Fill out our membership form
  • Join our Discord server
  • Attend our orientation session
What games do you support?

We currently support:

  • Valorant
  • League of Legends
  • Mobile Legends: Bang Bang
  • Call of Duty: Mobile
When are your tournaments held?

We organize the Pylon Cup each semester, typically during:

  • First semester: August-September
  • Second semester: January-February
Do I need to be a pro gamer to join?

Not at all! We welcome players of all skill levels. Our community focuses on improvement, learning, and having fun together.

What benefits do members receive?

Members enjoy:

  • Access to exclusive tournaments
  • Training sessions with experienced players
  • Network with other gaming enthusiasts
  • Opportunity to represent PUP in competitions
