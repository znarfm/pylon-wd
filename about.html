About Us

Pylon Esports is the official esports organization of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), dedicated to fostering a vibrant gaming community and providing competitive platforms for students.

Our Mission

To empower PUP students by promoting esports excellence, teamwork, and personal development through organized gaming events and community engagement.

Our Vision

To establish a recognized and inclusive esports community within PUP that nurtures talent, fosters camaraderie, and contributes positively to the broader gaming industry.

Our History

Since its inception, Pylon Esports has been instrumental in organizing events such as the Pylon Cup, offering students opportunities to showcase their skills in various gaming tournaments. The organization also hosts orientations and workshops to introduce new members to the esports community and provide guidance on balancing academics with gaming pursuits

Join Us

Pylon Esports welcomes all PUP students interested in gaming, whether casual or competitive. Members can participate in tournaments, attend workshops, and contribute to a supportive community that values both gaming and academic success.
